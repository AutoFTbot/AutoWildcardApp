Privacy Policy

AutoWildcard - Cloudflare Wildcard DNS Setup Tool
Last Updated: January 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AutoWildcard. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application.

Important: AutoWildcard does NOT collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers. All data is stored locally on your device with encryption.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Cloudflare Email: Your Cloudflare account email address
  • Global API Key: Your Cloudflare Global API Key for authentication
  • Domain Names: Domain names you want to configure
  • Custom Subdomains: Optional custom subdomain configurations

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • FCM Token: Firebase Cloud Messaging token for push notifications
  • Device Information: Android version, device model (for compatibility)
  • App Usage: Basic app usage statistics (locally stored)

2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Browsing history
  • Financial information

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To authenticate with Cloudflare API using your credentials
  • DNS Configuration: To setup wildcard DNS for your domains
  • Local Storage: To save your login credentials for future use (encrypted)
  • Push Notifications: To send important updates via FCM (optional)
Note: Your Cloudflare API Key is NEVER sent to our servers. It is only used to communicate directly with Cloudflare API from your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your Android device using:

  • EncryptedSharedPreferences: AndroidX Security Crypto library
  • Encryption Method: AES256-GCM encryption
  • Master Key: Android Keystore System
  • Storage Location: /data/data/com.autowildcard/shared_prefs/ (encrypted)

4.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • Secure communication with Cloudflare API (HTTPS only)
  • Auto-cleanup of temporary workers after setup
  • No logging of sensitive information

5. Third-Party Services

AutoWildcard uses the following third-party services:

5.1 Cloudflare API

  • Used for DNS configuration and domain management
  • Your credentials are sent directly to Cloudflare (not through our servers)
  • Subject to Cloudflare Privacy Policy

5.2 Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

  • Used for push notifications
  • FCM token is generated automatically by Firebase SDK
  • Subject to Firebase Privacy Policy

5.3 Google AdMob

  • Used for displaying advertisements
  • May collect device identifiers for ad personalization
  • Subject to Google Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

Your data is stored on your device until you:

  • Logout from the app (data is deleted)
  • Uninstall the app (all data is removed)
  • Clear app data from Android settings

We do not retain any data on external servers because we don't collect it in the first place.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your stored data (locally on your device)
  • Delete: Remove your data by logging out or uninstalling the app
  • Modify: Update your credentials anytime
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications in Android settings

8. Children's Privacy

AutoWildcard is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending a push notification (if enabled)
  • Displaying an in-app notice

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Permissions

AutoWildcard requires the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET: To communicate with Cloudflare API
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check internet connectivity
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send push notifications (Android 13+)

We do NOT request unnecessary permissions like camera, contacts, or location.
