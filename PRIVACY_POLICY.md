# Privacy Policy for AutoWildcard

**Last Updated: February 5, 2026**

## Introduction

AutoWildcard ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide
- **Cloudflare Credentials**: Email and API Key for Cloudflare integration
  - Stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
  - Never transmitted to our servers
  - Only used to communicate with Cloudflare API

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, crash reports
- **Advertising Data**: Collected by Google AdMob (see below)

## How We Use Your Information

### Cloudflare Integration
- Your Cloudflare credentials are used solely to:
  - Authenticate with Cloudflare API
  - Manage your domains and DNS records
  - Configure wildcard DNS settings
- Credentials are encrypted and stored only on your device
- We never have access to your credentials

### App Functionality
- Usage data helps us improve app performance
- Crash reports help us fix bugs and issues

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process:
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Device information
- Ad interaction data
- Location data (approximate)

For more information about how Google uses data:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google AdMob Privacy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

### Cloudflare API
When you use our app, it communicates directly with Cloudflare API:
- Your credentials are sent directly to Cloudflare
- We do not intercept or store API communications
- Cloudflare's privacy policy applies: [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All user data is stored locally on your device
- Cloudflare credentials are encrypted using Android Keystore
- No data is transmitted to our servers

### Security Measures
- Industry-standard encryption for sensitive data
- Secure communication with Cloudflare API (HTTPS)
- No cloud backup of credentials

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access and Deletion
- You can delete your credentials anytime by logging out
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

### Advertising Choices
You can opt-out of personalized advertising:
- Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- Or visit: [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
